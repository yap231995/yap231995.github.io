Biography


Academic Pursuit

Currently, I am working as a Research Assistant at Temasek Laboratories @ NTU (TL@NTU). Concurrently, I am pursuing my PhD in Mathematical Sciences at the School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences, Nanyang Technological University. My research is supervised by Thomas Peyrin and co-supervised by Shivam Bhasin. As part of my research, I am also affiliated with SYLLAB and PACE. I graduated with a Bachelor in Science (Mathematics) with Honors from the National University of Singapore from 2016 to 2020.
